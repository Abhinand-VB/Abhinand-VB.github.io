Online Treasure Hunt

A multi-step riddle I designed. Have a go before you reveal the solution.

  1. Two characters, one difference. The assassin is Agent 47 from Hitman (number 47); the destroyer deity is tied to Universe 5 in Dragon Ball (number 5). Their difference: 47 − 5 = 42.
  2. 42 points to a famous answer. In The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, 42 is the answer to the ultimate question of life, the universe, and everything — so the three blanks fill in as Life, The Universe, and Everything.
  3. The portrait is a unit. It's Heinrich Hertz — "hertz" measures frequency. Searching for the universe's frequency leads to 432 Hz, the code for the QR number-lock.
  4. Unlock the finale. Feeding 432 into the QR sequence reveals the punchline.
Final answer: Directed by Michael Bay — an everything-explodes gag to cap the chain.
